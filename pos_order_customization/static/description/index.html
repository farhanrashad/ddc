POS Order Customization

With the help of this module, POS User has an option to customize product/order on the basis of customer needs and also charge some extra amount as per product customization. This module is good for such a scenario when some product has options like extra cheese, extra butter, extra onions, Vegetarian or non-vegetarian etc and POS user asked the customer to customize his order as per options available for the product.

Features:

  • Seller can set customization options/questions with price in the backend.
  • Seller can customize order any time after adding products to the cart.
  • Price of the product will be auto adjusted on the basis of selected customization options.
  • The selected customization options will also be shown in the cart
  • The selected customization options will be printed on both receipts (POSBOX or POS Ticket) .
  • The selected customization options for product will also be saved in backend.
